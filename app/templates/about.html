About This Blog

Mission

The purpose of the bearistotle blog is two-fold:

  1. To provide a place for our contributors to develop their understanding of the key concepts, issues, and practices in their domain.
  2. To provide a way for the creator to practice coding.

Contributors

  • Jake Heminway
  • Carly Heminway

Origin

This blog started as a practice project by the creator for Miguel Grinburg's Flask Mega-Tutorial.
